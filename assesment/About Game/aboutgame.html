About MANHUNT

Manhunt is an intense stealth FPS escape game.

Set on a remote, mysterious island, you must carefully avoid enemies.

Your goal is to collect scraps scattered across the island.

Use these scraps to build a spaceship.

Only by escaping the island can you survive and leave the world behind.
